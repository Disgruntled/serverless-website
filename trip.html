I needed a change of pace

I've been fantasizing about the west coast for years. After a hard night at the pub with a good friend of mine who just decided to move to Berlin I was inspired to do go after my dream.

I'm very fortunate to have a partner who would indulge such a fantasy(thx Lara!) and who is also the perfect adventure buddy.

After a very frank conversation with my boss ("I'm moving, and you can decide what this means to you"), we packed the car and headed west to find a new life.
